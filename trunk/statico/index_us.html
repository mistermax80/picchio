La Villa Hotel

The hotel ‘La Villa’, situated in a strategic position, offers its guests a breath-taking view of Lake Bracciano to be enjoyed in absolute peace and the possibility to reach the center of the village and main services in a few minutes.

The new management and the young and cheerful atmosphere transform the hotel into a “country house on the lake”, making it a nice place for a holiday and relaxation. Its typical bedrooms, some of which are equipped with a fireplace and panoramic balcony, remind one of the pleasures of ordinary life, and rhythms we are no longer familiar with, while the lake’s backwash and the sea-gulls’ glide mark time.

La Villa hotel represents an ideal solution for those who wish to include sport, relaxation, amusement and culture in their holiday, thanks not only to the sailing, canoeing and windsurfing courses, the sailing clubs and beaches with facilities, but also to the museums, castles and ruins which enrich our artistic and cultural heritage.
