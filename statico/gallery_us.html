The Hotel

The hotel consists of nine bright, cozy and quiet rooms. Some have a view over the lake and a fireplace inside; they are all equipped with air conditioning, TV, internet connection and Wi-Fi, heating, bathroom with shower or bath.





The Restaurant

The property has a restaurant to taste the best of the local cuisine of Lazio.





The Territory

The hotel is located a few meters from Lake Bracciano. On the shores of the lake rises, plus Anguillara, Bracciano and Trevignano Romano.
